Specialised Services

Nadi Parikshan — Pulse Diagnosis

The cornerstone of Ayurvedic diagnosis. Dr. G.S. Jadon uses traditional three-finger pulse reading (Vata · Pitta · Kapha) at the radial artery to diagnose doshic imbalances, organ conditions, and body constitution (Prakriti/Vikriti) — a completely non-invasive, root-cause diagnostic method.

Classical Procedures

Panchakarma & Therapies

01

Kati / Greeva Basti

Warm medicated oil retained in a dough dam on spine. Deeply effective for lumbar spondylosis and disc prolapse.

02

Patra Pinda Sveda

Bolus massage using heated herbal leaves. For joint pain, arthritis, and neuromuscular conditions.

03

Jalaukavacharana

Leech therapy — powerful Raktamokshana for varicose veins, chronic skin and vascular conditions.

04

Herbal Churna Therapy

Custom-blended powders for संधि विकार, उदर रोग, अपस्मार — prepared fresh for each patient.

05

Kshar Sutra

Para-surgical Ayurvedic technique for Piles, Fistula-in-Ano, and Fissure. No relapse, minimal recovery time.

06

Uttara Basti & Yoni Dhavan

Specialised Ayurvedic procedures for female reproductive health, infertility, PCOS/PCOD management.
